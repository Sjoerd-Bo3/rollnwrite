## Description (≤4000)

```
Roll'n Write is a clean, automatic scorecard for roll-and-write dice games on
iPhone and iPad. Tap to cross out spaces — the app enforces the official rules
strictly and computes your score for you. It's a pure scorecard: bring your own
dice and game, and it handles the bookkeeping.

FEATURES
• Strict rule enforcement — illegal moves are blocked; locks, penalties and
  game-end are handled automatically.
• Automatic scoring — every row and bonus is totted up live.
• Tap-to-undo — tap your last mark to take it back, or use the undo button.
• Two-player mode — a mirrored second board for the player across the table.
• Official rules included, right in the app.
• Universal — designed for every iPhone size and iPad.
• Private by design — no accounts, no ads, no tracking. Everything stays on
  your device.

Includes the Big Points and classic scoring variants, plus more roll-and-write
games on the way.

—
Unofficial fan project. Not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any
game publisher. All game names and trademarks belong to their respective owners.
```

> In the "names-it" version, replace "the Big Points and classic scoring
> variants" with the specific Qwixx variant names — at your trademark discretion.
