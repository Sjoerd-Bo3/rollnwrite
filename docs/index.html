Roll’n Write

A clean, automatic scorecard for roll-and-write dice games.
iPhone & iPad.

Tap to cross out spaces — the app enforces the official rules strictly and computes your score automatically. It’s a pure scorecard: you bring your own dice and game, it handles the bookkeeping.

Strict rules

Illegal moves are blocked; locks, penalties, and game-end are enforced for you.

Automatic scoring

Every row and bonus is totted up live — no maths, no mistakes.

Tap to undo

Tap your last mark to take it back, or use the undo button.

Two players

A mirrored second board for the player across the table.

Rules included

The official rules for each game, right in the app.

Private by design

No accounts, no ads, no tracking. Everything stays on your device.

More roll-and-write games are on the way.
