Privacy Policy — Roll'n Write

Last updated: 29 June 2026

Roll'n Write (“the app”) is a digital scorecard for roll-and-write dice games. Your privacy is simple to explain: the app collects no personal data and sends nothing off your device.

What we collect

Nothing. The app has no accounts, no sign-in, and makes no network requests.

What is stored, and where

Your in-progress scorecards and per-game settings (such as the dice-colour mapping) are saved only on your device, using the system's local storage. This data never leaves your device, is not transmitted to us or any third party, and is removed when you delete the app.

Analytics, tracking, and advertising

The app contains no analytics, no tracking, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs. It does not use the Advertising Identifier and does not track you across apps or websites.

Data from children

Because the app collects no data at all, it collects no data from children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new date.
