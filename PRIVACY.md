# Privacy Policy — Roll'n Write

_Last updated: 2026-06-29_

Roll'n Write ("the app") is a digital scorecard for roll-and-write dice games.
Your privacy is simple to explain: **the app collects no personal data and sends
nothing off your device.**

## What we collect

**Nothing.** The app has no accounts, no sign-in, and makes no network requests.

## What is stored, and where

Your in-progress scorecards and the per-game settings (such as the dice-colour
mapping) are saved **only on your device**, using the system's local storage
(`UserDefaults`). This data:

- never leaves your device,
- is not transmitted to us or to any third party,
- is removed when you delete the app.

## Analytics, tracking, and advertising

The app contains **no analytics, no tracking, no advertising, and no third-party
SDKs**. It does not use the Advertising Identifier and does not track you across
apps or websites.

## Data from children

Because the app collects no data at all, it collects no data from children.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with
a new "Last updated" date.
